Core Histology Services

Southeast Pathology Instrument Service is a leader in providing factory trained service on histo-pathology instrumentation in hospitals and independent laboratories throughout the Southeast and abroad. Specializing in repair and calibration of the core histology instruments including:

  • Microtomes
  • Tissue Processors
  • Cryostats
  • Coverslippers
  • H&E Stainers
  • IHC Stainers
  • Embedding Centers
  • Slide & Cassette Printers

Service Info

Our team of 9 highly trained technicians can get your equipment up and running with little down time in between. We maintain a healthy parts inventory to keep the need for timely and expensive repeat visits to a minimum. We also have a supply of loaner instruments available should you experience an urgent need due to a failing instrument. Please note that we are certified in refrigeration repair and fully outfitted to service any Cryostat cooling malfunction you may be experiencing.
