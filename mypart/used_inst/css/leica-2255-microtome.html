Leica RM 2255 Microtome

Instrument Description

The Leica RM 2255 microtome is a completely motorized microtome that can also be utilized as a manual microtome due to its counterbalance hand wheel system. Main uses are for sectioning paraffin and resin embedded specimens and some non-standard materials. The Leica 2255 can be used in range of laboratories environments such as routine and research settings.

Features include 70mm vertical stroke, precision orientation system, high powered step-motor system, predefined cutting modes.
